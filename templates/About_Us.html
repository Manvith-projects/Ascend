Introduction

Welcome to ASCEND - Advanced Support for Career Exploration and Navigating Directions. We are committed to providing AI-powered career guidance to help students make informed decisions about their future.

What is ASCEND?

ASCEND is an AI-driven career counseling platform that provides personalized career recommendations, mentorship programs, and resource portals for students at different academic levels.

Why ASCEND?

Many students face career confusion due to limited guidance. ASCEND bridges this gap by offering AI-powered insights and expert mentorship aligned with NEP 2020.

Key Features

  • Personalized AI-driven career guidance
  • Mentorship programs with professionals and chatbots
  • Extensive career resources and market trend insights
  • Resume-based interview practice and job recommendations
  • Startup ideas and job market analysis
  • Public pathways for government career options

Our Vision

To empower students with AI-driven career guidance, helping them navigate their academic and professional journey with confidence.
